Welcome to Provincial Books, we are an independently owned and operate bookstore catering to all of your literary needs. We know that sometimes finding the perfect book can be a challenge, that is why we have an online store that allows you to purchase books from the comfort of your own home for your convenience. We also have a from that allows you to request a book and if we do not have it, we will order it and allow you to come pick it up at your leisure.

If you come to our physical location we have reading areas scattered throughout the store for you to find a secluded spot to enjoy a recent purchase or to browse through our periodicals stocked daily. We also have free WiFi for any and all who need it so you will be able to stay connected no matter what.
